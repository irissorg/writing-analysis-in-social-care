Working together apart

Author: Josie Vallely

Published: August 2020

This resource defines attachment, examines the components and evidence underlying attachment, and the patterns of attachment behaviour.

There is a summary of current policy implications, and this goes alongside practice points and video perspectives from Kenny Toshack (Adoption and Fostering Team, local authority) and Corry McDonald (parenting and assessment service, North Ayrshire Health and Social Care Partnership).
